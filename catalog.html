FREE RESOURCE

How to measure & install windscreen.

  • · Measure tension-bar to tension-bar — not post-to-post.
  • · No panel longer than 50–60′. Plan seams to land at a fence post.
  • · Standard fence heights: 3′ / 6′ / 8′ / 10′ / 12′.
  • · Measure from valley of top diamond down to valley of bottom diamond.
  • · Hog-ring or plastic tie every 12″–18″ (every grommet).
  • · Material has stretch — install on hill-grade fences fine.

The same notes we hand to our crews. Call or email for the full sheet, or read the original on the source site.
