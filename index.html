HOW WE WORK

Wholesale.
Manufacturer.
Contractor.

Three roles, one workshop. We sell raw goods to other installers, fabricate finished pieces from inventory, and put it on the fence ourselves across Greater LA. Anywhere outside that radius — we ship.

  1. STEP 01

    CALL OR EMAIL

    800-773-4657 or njpsports@earthlink.net. Tell us the fence height, run length, and what you’re trying to block.

  2. STEP 02

    SAMPLES & QUOTE

    We send physical fabric samples and a written quote. Mesh weights, vinyl colors, hem details, grommet spacing — the whole spec.

  3. STEP 03

    FABRICATE

    Cut, hem, grommet, tape, label. Done on the floor in Glendale. Logos and printed graphics handled in-house.

  4. STEP 04

    INSTALL OR SHIP

    Install crew across Greater LA. Worldwide shipping otherwise — rolled, palletized, freight-ready.
